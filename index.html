About Me

Hello! I'm Rhythm Yeasir, a dedicated and versatile Computer Science and Engineering student at the University of Asia Pacific with a strong passion for game development and artificial intelligence. As an indie game developer, I specialize in creating immersive 2D platformers with advanced mechanics, blending technical precision with creative design using tools like Unity and C#. I'm committed to building gameplay experiences that are both engaging and emotionally resonant.

In addition to development, I'm a self-taught music producer and sound designer focused on crafting immersive audio that enhances narrative and atmosphere. From ambient soundscapes to rhythmically intense sequences, I use digital audio workstations, synthesizers, field recordings, and layered effects to shape rich, cinematic audio experiences that bring virtual worlds to life.

My technical skill set includes a wide range of programming languages and tools such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, C#, C/C++, Java, Unity, and Godot. I'm a lifelong learner who thrives on exploring new technologies and constantly evolving my creative and technical abilities. Outside of development, I enjoy experimenting with new ideas and disciplines to stay inspired and adaptable.
